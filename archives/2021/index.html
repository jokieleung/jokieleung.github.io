Bio

My name is Zujie Liang (梁祖杰). I’m a second-year master of philosophy student at Sun Yat-Sen University (SYSU). I am currently a Research Intern at Microsoft, working on Conversation AI.

My research interests include Deep Learning algorithms and applications, especially Natural Language Processing/Generation, De-biased Learning and Computer Vision.
